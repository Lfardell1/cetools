When to apply certain excesses

Standard XS Applies in nearly all cases unless a coverage specifically says it does not. When we have both contents and building damage we'll always choose the highest one and apply it
Unoccupied XS Unoccupied XS applies when a house has been unnocupied for more than 60 days in the policy. occupancy is defined by staying at the property for 2 consecutive nights This applies on top of the standard XS
Water This excess applies in addition to any other excess if the insured makes a claim for loss or damage caused by leaks in or from pipes or water containers
Malicious Damage This will apply to any claim for malicious damage by tenants and apply on top of the standard XS
Theft This will apply to any claim for theft by tenants and apply on top of the standard XS
Earthquake and tsunami excess This excess applies in addition to any other excess if there is a claim for loss or damage as a result of an earthquake or tsunami
Loss of Rent When the insured makes a claim for loss of rent - tenant default a special XS applies and no other XS applies
Claims History XS This will sometimes show up when our policies department has decided that the insured is risky to insure (too many claims). This normally applies when the insured has had 3 or more than 3 claims in 2-3 years but can be up to our discretion (refer to HSO) This XS will always apply on top of the standard XS except for portable valuables claims
Suncorp & GIO motor burnout $100 XS These policies have motor burnout as an optional extra and they have their own XS applicable There is currently an error in claim center where this doesn't show up. Always be on the lookout for this and apply when needed
Portable valuables (General & Specified effects) Whenever an insured is claiming under portables and the standard XS does not apply
Enthusiast items Applied when insured is only claiming under the additional cover 'Enthusiast items' on the shannons policy. Applies on its own
Off-shore XS This will sometimes appear when the insured has their property insured on an island that makes it difficult / more risky to insure. This will apply on top of the standard XS
